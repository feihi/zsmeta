ABOUT US

OGBC was launched to serve as an integrated services hub with a focus on cultivating a unified entrepreneurial community built on a foundation of innovation and open collaboration.

Our mission is to bring together entrepreneurs, investors, and professionals from both the Web3 and traditional industries, in a joint effort towards creating a thriving Web 3.0 digital economy.

Headquartered in Singapore, we also have offices in Kuala Lumpur, Hong Kong and Melbourne, with a global network of partners and clients spanning across multiple continents, including Asia, Europe, North America, and beyond. We serve as a conduit for Asia’s blossoming blockchain space, bridging the cultural and regulatory gaps between emerging blockchain communities, governmental bodies and traditional businesses.

OGBC offers a multitude of product and service offerings necessary for both new and established players, from Blockchain-as-a-Service, strategy consulting, compliance, fund management, serviced offices and private concierge services. As new developments and technologies emerge constantly in the fast-paced world of blockchain, we also offer strong networking and educational opportunities for our members to stay ahead of the curve, drive innovations and lead the way in the growth of the industry.
We believe that our collective strength, expertise, network and resources can be fully harnessed to bring about impactful changes, and provide valuable guidance and support to entrepreneurs and businesses looking to navigate the rapidly-evolving world of blockchain technology.
